Dataset Description: Supply Chain Risk and Performance Indicators
This dataset offers a comprehensive view of supply chain operations, enriched with both operational metrics and external contextual indicators. It captures over 113,000 entries, each representing a supply chain instance characterised by warehouse, logistics, supplier, and environmental variables.

The dataset includes:

Operational Performance Indicators: Metrics such as inventory levels, shipping costs, lead times, handling equipment availability, and delivery deviations.

Supplier Attributes: Reliability scores, IDs, geographic information (including country codes), and economic context from their respective locations.

Risk-related Variables: Indicators like delay probabilities, disruption likelihood scores, route risk levels, and customs clearance durations—key to assessing logistical vulnerabilities.

The inclusion of a risk classification label and delivery time deviation makes this dataset especially suitable for training and validating predictive models focused on risk assessment and supply chain resilience.This rich combination of internal and external factors enables multifaceted analyses, from forecasting delivery performance to mapping systemic vulnerabilities across supply networks.
